Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 13, 2026

Welcome to Tiki Taka Cup. Your privacy is highly important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you log in using Google Authentication, we collect basic profile information provided by Google, including:

  • Your email address
  • Your public display name
  • Your profile picture

2. How We Use Your Information

We use this information strictly to:

  • Authenticate your identity and allow you to log in.
  • Create and maintain your user profile.
  • Display your display name on the global match leaderboard.

3. Data Storage & Third Parties

Your authentication and profile data is securely managed using Supabase (our database infrastructure provider) and protected by industry-standard encryption. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties.

4. Data Deletion

If you wish to have your account and associated leaderboard data permanently deleted, please contact us at juan.carlos.1800@gmail.com, and we will process your request promptly.
